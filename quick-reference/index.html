⚠ Important Field Notes
TopicNote
T350 / T&E StipT350 and Premium Time are always billed at stip factor of 1.0 — do not adjust the multiplier regardless of time window.
Rock RemovalAll rock items (T50, T51, T51H, T52) require a photo with placard before backfill. No placard = no payment.
Pneumatic ToolPneumatic tool inside the trench requires CCI approval before proceeding.
Steel Main WeldsG26AS, G27AS, etc. do not include welds in the LF price. Bill each weld separately under GT90A–GT90F.
Restoration Suffix (Micro)Roadway / curb / sidewalk Micro items — A = installation only · R = removal + installation · I = incremental (combines with other suffixes). Base T1–T9 & T21–T23 = removal only; T30–T32 = installation only.
Service Suffix KeySuffix: 1=Manhattan (≤30') · 2=Queens/Bronx (≤50') · 3=Westchester (≤65')  |  S=steel  |  I=≤ territory length · II=> territory length
